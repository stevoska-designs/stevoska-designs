About Project

The Inner Circle is a wellness platform created for people who spend most of their day working at a desk. It offers a supportive environment for improving physical health, emotional balance, and long-term well-being.

The platform includes personal training, wellness circles, and 1:1 coaching, giving users a flexible way to stay active and reduce stress.
